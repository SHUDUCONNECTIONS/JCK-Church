About Jesus Christ Kingdom

History

Founded in Faith

Jesus Christ Kingdom (JCK) is based in SAMCOR PARK, Pretoria. Established in May 2012 under the leadership of Apostle Alloyd N Moyo, JCK prepares God's people for the second coming of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Under the tagline "Sons and Daughters of Revelation", JCK has grown into a vibrant church community that reaches beyond Sunday services — actively caring for the vulnerable, training leaders, and transforming Mamelodi one life at a time.

In 2016, the church launched JCK Life Changers (Reg. 174-699-NPO) as its community development arm, formalising work across education, health care, farming, and dignity.

Vision

To contribute to community outreach and development by creating an environment of hope, wherein every challenged person can be strengthened, assisted, and supported with the love of God.

Mission

To manage a Kingdom that can serve individuals with the love of God by creating lasting solutions to poverty and hunger in the poorest communities.
