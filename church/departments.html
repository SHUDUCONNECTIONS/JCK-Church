Church Structure

Our Departments

Each department plays a vital role in the body of Christ at JCK. Click any department to learn more.

Pastors' Department

Pastors have seemingly endless responsibilities — responding to members, delivering sermons, leading Bible study groups, and performing rites. They work hand in hand with deacons, evangelists and Wisemen in the smooth running of the church.

  • Preaching, teaching and Bible study leadership
  • Baptisms, weddings, baby dedications and Lord's Supper
  • Pre-marital counselling and cell groups
  • Overseeing worship services and committee attendance
  • Must be married and ordained by the Apostle/Overseer
Executive Committee

The Executive Committee guides strategy, oversight, and governance of the church.

  • Strategic planning and long-term goal setting
  • Monitoring performance and accountability
  • Policy development and compliance
  • Members must demonstrate integrity, diverse expertise, and ethical conduct
  • Confidentiality and discretion in all sensitive matters
Healing and Delivery

The Wise Men are the spiritual security of the church, ensuring JCK does not experience spiritual attacks. Their gifts include deliverance, healing, and prophecy.

  • Praying for people by laying on of hands and conducting mass prayers
  • Conducting mountain prayers and baptisms
  • Serving alongside and supporting the Apostle
  • Must be approved, ordained and proven to live a life worthy of the Gospel
Choir Department

The choir ministers in praise and worship — a direct communication with Heaven. Members must be clean and pure, understanding who they are in the Kingdom.

  • Choir practice: every Friday 18:30 – 20:30
  • All Sunday choir members arrive 08:00 – 08:30 for prayer
  • Must be a fulltime, born-again, water and Holy Spirit baptised JCK member
  • New members begin as backup singers during a waiting period
  • Responsible for care and maintenance of all musical instruments
Ushering Department

Ushers are the mirrors of the Kingdom — leading, directing, and serving members and visitors in all church activities.

  • Must be filled with the Holy Spirit, self-confident, and willing to serve
  • Attend every church service, Sunday prayer, outreach, and whole-night services
  • Two attires: formal Ushering uniform and Deliverance attire
  • Vision to open a bank account to assist ushers in need
Evangelists Department

Evangelists are ordained by Apostle to win souls, hold cell groups, provide counselling, and intercede for the church.

  • Opening all JCK services spiritually
  • House-to-house evangelism and hospital/prison visitation
  • Holding funeral services, all-night prayers, and Holy Communion
  • Caring for widows, orphans, and new members
  • Must understand the spiritual anointing and protocol of JCK
Children's Department

The Children's Ministry equips children of all ages with the word of God, running concurrently with the Sunday worship service.

  • Age groups: 1–3, 4–6, 6–8, and 9–12 year olds
  • Programme includes prayer, Bible lessons, memory verses, songs, and physical activities
  • Activities: drama, outings, video days, sports day, Bible quiz, camps
  • Goal: raise a godly, Spirit-filled youth who will not be ashamed of the Gospel
Security Department

As Apostle says, the Church is a Spiritual Parliament — and like any parliament, it requires security and protocol.

  • Stationed at entrance/exit doors, working with ushers
  • Guard the movement of the Man of God throughout services
  • Must be first to arrive and last to leave
  • Alert, flexible, fit, and able to communicate with other units
  • Must be a baptised JCK member, honest, and willing to serve
