Who We Are

Changing Lives,
One Community at a Time

Established in August 2016 within Jesus Christ Kingdom Church, JCK Life Changers was born from the church leadership's observation of community need. We address education and social issues affecting children, youth, men, and women in Mamelodi and Mpumalanga by establishing programmes in poor communities.

Mission: To establish and manage an organisation that can serve individuals with the love of God by creating lasting solutions to poverty and hunger in the poorest communities.

Our Vision

Create a mentality of change, perseverance, and innovative thinking that will sustain and continuously improve the community environment.

Attain a productive and active attitude of change and growth approached from a mental perspective.

Enhance visionary and mental participation in community affairs for all people, preparing for a stable and self-sustainable future.
