Delivering food and essential supplies directly to housebound elderly community members — because no one should be left behind.

About the Programme

Many elderly community members are unable to leave their homes due to illness, disability, or age. They cannot travel to food distribution points or community kitchens. They are invisible to programmes that require people to come to them. JCK Life Changers goes to them instead.

Our Door 2 Door Food Parcels programme identifies housebound elderly people in Mamelodi and delivers food parcels and essential supplies directly to their homes. We leave no one behind — not because of a slogan, but because of a conviction.

What's in a Parcel

  • Staple foods — mealie meal, rice, and flour
  • Protein — canned beans, fish, and lentils
  • Cooking oil and salt
  • Long-life milk and tea
  • Hygiene basics — soap and washing powder
  • Fresh vegetables when available from our farming programmes

How We Find Those in Need

We identify recipients through church members, community leaders, and referrals from neighbours. Our volunteers build ongoing relationships with the families and individuals they serve, learning their specific needs and checking on their wellbeing during each delivery.

These deliveries are not anonymous drops — they are visits. Our volunteers sit with recipients, pray with them, and report back on any additional needs. This community intelligence helps us respond more effectively and holistically.

Regularity and Consistency

We are committed to regular, consistent delivery — not a one-time gesture. Recipients on our programme can count on us. This consistency is what separates JCK Life Changers from event-based charity. We come back, month after month, because hunger comes back month after month.
