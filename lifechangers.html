Our Community Work

8 Active Outreach Programmes

JCK Life Changers runs 8 dedicated outreach programmes across Mamelodi and Mpumalanga — from farming and school gardens to dignity drives and prison outreach. Each programme has its own story, its own impact, and its own way to get involved.
