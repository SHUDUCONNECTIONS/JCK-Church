What We Do

Serving God. Serving People.

Jesus Christ Kingdom prepares God's people for the second coming of our Lord. We serve through word, worship, and community outreach.

Spiritual Growth

Weekly services, Bible study, cell groups, and prayer nights. We equip every member to walk in the fullness of their calling in Christ.

Community Outreach

Through JCK Life Changers NPO, we run feeding schemes, farming projects, home-based care, and dignity drives across Mamelodi.

Children's Ministry

A structured programme that raises children in God's word, building a godly generation equipped for life and eternity.

Praise & Worship

Our choir ministers before the Lord in spirit and truth, preparing hearts to receive the word of God every service.

Farming Initiative

Equipping communities in Mpumalanga and Mamelodi to grow food, fight hunger, and build economic independence.

Evangelism

House-to-house ministry, hospital visits, prison outreach, and winning souls for the Kingdom of Jesus Christ.
